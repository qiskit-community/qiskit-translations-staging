ElectronicEnergy¶

class ElectronicEnergy(electronic_integrals, energy_shift=None, nuclear_repulsion_energy=None, reference_energy=None)[source]¶

Bases: qiskit_nature.properties.second_quantization.electronic.integrals.integral_property.IntegralProperty

The ElectronicEnergy property.

This is the main property of any electronic structure problem. It constructs the Hamiltonian whose eigenvalue is the target of a later used Quantum algorithm.

Note that this Property computes purely the electronic energy (possibly minus additional shifts introduced via e.g. classical transformers). However, for convenience it provides a storage location for the nuclear repulsion energy. If available, this information will be used during the call of interpret to provide the electronic, nuclear and total energy components in the result object.

Tham số

  • electronic_integrals (List[ElectronicIntegrals]) -- a dictionary mapping the # body terms to the corresponding ElectronicIntegrals.

  • energy_shift (Optional[Dict[str, complex]]) -- an optional dictionary of energy shifts.

  • nuclear_repulsion_energy (Optional[float]) -- the optional nuclear repulsion energy.

  • reference_energy (Optional[float]) -- an optional reference energy (such as the HF energy).
